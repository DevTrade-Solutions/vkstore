Flower Care Instructions

Keep your flowers fresh and beautiful longer with these tips

Recut Stems

Recut stems diagonally every ~3 days for better water absorption.

Clean Vase

Use a clean vase and don't overcrowd your flowers.

Fresh Water

Fill vase ¾ with room-temp water and change every 3 days.

Keep Cool

Keep flowers cool and refrigerate if away for extended periods.
